i. The Reason We're Here Where Most Brands Get Stuck

You're posting. Nothing is landing.

01 / Identity— soft drift

Your brand looks familiar. Not memorable.

Same fonts, the same neutral palette everyone reaches for, the same "minimal" feed. Quietly blending into a feed designed to skip you. Beautiful brands choose a side — yours hasn't yet.

02 / Content— quietly stalling

Posting consistently. Going nowhere.

You're treating content like a checklist instead of a story. No hooks, no rhythm, no point of view — just three reels a week and a vague hope the algorithm rewards you. It rarely does.

03 / Strategy— without a compass

No idea who you're really talking to.

The work is eating your weekends.

You started the business to do the thing you're great at. Now you spend Sunday nights writing captions, batching reels, and second-guessing your hashtags. That isn't growth, that's exhaustion.

vii. FAQ Everything Else

The questions people actually ask.

i. Why not just hire a content creator on Fiverr? +
You can. They'll make pretty posts. They won't fix the underlying brand strategy, audience clarity, or system that turns content into clients. I do all of it together — because aesthetics without strategy is just expensive decoration.
ii. How fast will I see results? +
Honest answer — week 4 onward. Weeks 1–3 we're building the foundation. From week 4 you'll feel the shift in clarity, voice, and visual quality. Real growth signals (saves, DMs, leads) typically show by day 60–90 if you stay consistent.
iii. Do you guarantee follower growth? +
No. Anyone who guarantees follower counts is either selling you bots or selling you hopium. I guarantee a clear brand, a working content system, and the strategic foundation that makes growth possible. The algorithm and your consistency do the rest.
iv. Which platforms do you cover? +
Mainly Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn — that's where 95% of my clients live. I'll also help with YouTube Shorts and Pinterest if it fits the strategy. I won't spread you across seven platforms. Two done well beats five done badly.
v. Can you work with brands outside the Netherlands? +
Yes. I'm based in Rotterdam but work in EN with clients across the EU, UK, and US. Calls happen on Zoom, async work on Slack/Notion. Time zones haven't been a problem yet.
vi. What if I already have a brand identity? +
Even better. We'll audit it, sharpen what works, and rebuild what doesn't. Most "existing" brand identities are a logo and a vibe — not a system. The Social Engine package adapts to whatever foundation is already there.
